Introducing MedBot

Your AI-Health Assistant

Welcome to MedBot, an innovative health assistance website at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and personalized care. Crafted with Python, Django, Scikit-Learn, and TensorFlow, MedBot boasts two distinctive bots: ChatBot and PredBot.
The ChatBot, leveraging an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) and Bag-of-Words (BOW) deep learning model powered by TensorFlow, engages users in text-based conversations. True to its name, ChatBot interprets user input, providing tailored responses and addressing their health concerns with the precision of advanced neural network architecture.
Meanwhile, PredBot, backed by a robust voting classifier implemented with sci-kit learn, takes a proactive role. Users input their symptoms, and PredBot utilizes a data-driven approach to predict potential diseases. This ensures users receive valuable insights into their health, guiding them towards informed decisions.
MedBot is your virtual health companion, seamlessly integrating sophisticated models to offer personalized health assistance. Whether engaging in conversational queries with ChatBot or exploring symptom-based disease predictions with PredBot, MedBot is designed to empower users on their health journey.
