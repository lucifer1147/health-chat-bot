Basic Instructions Regarding PredBot:
  • PredBot will take inputs in the format of a form.
  • The form will be 16 pages long in which you will be checking the symptoms that apply to you.
  • There will be three action button:
    • Next: This SAVES your choices made on that page and moves you on to the next. It is necessary that you Click this to save your choices if you decide to make any changes as no other option does that.
    • Previous: This moves you to the previous page and DOES NOT SAVE your choices.
    • Previous: This RESETS your choices for the active page. But, it DOES NOT SAVE the resetted choices.
    • Get Results: This button will only be available on the 16th page and it takes you to the results page.
  • There will also be a nav bar at the bottom of the page to indicate which page step you are on. You may navigate to any other page from there

This is PredBot, the proactive health companion within MedBot's arsenal. Designed to empower users with valuable insights into their health, PredBot stands as a robust predictive force. Utilizing a powerful voting classifier built with sci-kit learn, this intelligent system comes to life when users input their symptoms. PredBot analyzes the data, employing a data-driven approach to inform users about potential diseases they might be facing. It's a decisive step towards early awareness and informed decision-making, making PredBot an invaluable tool in your health journey. Explore symptoms, discover possibilities, and take charge of your well-being with PredBot's advanced predictive capabilities.
